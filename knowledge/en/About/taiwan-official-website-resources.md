# Taiwan Official Website Resources

Taiwan.md Official Website Resources is Taiwan's most comprehensive index of official/authoritative websites, covering government institutions, cultural education, tourism and travel, technology and economy, media and news, and various other sectors.

## Resource Categories

### 🏛️ Official Institutions

- **Central Government**: Presidential Office, Executive Yuan, Legislative Yuan, Judicial Yuan, and other five-branch agencies
- **Ministries**: Ministry of Health and Welfare, Ministry of Labor, Ministry of Environment, Ministry of Agriculture, Ministry of National Defense, Ministry of Justice, etc.
- **Local Governments**: Six special municipalities (Taipei, New Taipei, Taichung, Kaohsiung, Tainan, Taoyuan)
- **Special Agencies**: National Development Council, Mainland Affairs Council, Council of Indigenous Peoples, etc.

### 🎭 Culture and Education

- **Museums**: National Palace Museum, National Museum of Taiwan History, National Museum of Natural Science, National Museum of Marine Biology & Aquarium, National Museum of Taiwan Literature
- **Top Universities**: National Taiwan University, National Tsing Hua University, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University, National Cheng Kung University, National Chengchi University, National Taiwan Normal University
- **Research Institutions**: Academia Sinica, National Science and Technology Council
- **Arts and Culture**: National Theater & Concert Hall, Weiwuying, Taiwan Creative Content Agency

### ✈️ Tourism and Travel

- **National Parks**: Yushan, Taroko, Kenting, Shei-Pa, Yangmingshan
- **Transportation**: Taiwan Railways, Taiwan High Speed Rail, Taiwan Tourist Shuttle
- **Official Agencies**: Tourism Bureau, National Immigration Agency, Ministry of Transportation and Communications, Central Weather Bureau

### 💼 Technology and Economy

- **Leading Companies**: Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC), United Microelectronics Corporation (UMC), Hon Hai Precision Industry (Foxconn), Delta Electronics
- **Research Institutions**: Institute for Information Industry (III), National Applied Research Laboratories, Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI)
- **Financial Institutions**: Taiwan Stock Exchange, Taipei Exchange, Financial Supervisory Commission
- **Government Agencies**: Ministry of Economic Affairs, Bureau of Foreign Trade, Small and Medium Enterprise Administration

### 📰 Media and News

- **Official Media**: Central News Agency, Public Television Service (PTS), Radio Taiwan International, Focus Taiwan
- **Mainstream Media**: United Daily News, Liberty Times, TVBS, Sanlih E-Television, Eastern Broadcasting Company (EBC)
- **Digital Media**: The News Lens, The Reporter, CommonWealth Magazine

### 🏥 Healthcare and Medicine

- **Government Agencies**: Centers for Disease Control, National Health Insurance Administration
- **Medical Centers**: National Taiwan University Hospital, Veterans General Hospital, Chang Gung Medical Foundation

### 🤝 NGO / Civil Society

- **Human Rights Organizations**: Taiwan Association for Human Rights
- **Environmental Groups**: Environmental Information Center, Society of Wilderness
- **Digital Rights**: Open Culture Foundation
- **Media Monitoring**: Taiwan FactCheck Center

### 🎭 Arts and Cultural Venues

- **Art Museums**: Taipei Fine Arts Museum, Kaohsiung Museum of Fine Arts
- **Performance Venues**: National Taichung Theater
- **Cultural Parks**: Songshan Cultural and Creative Park, Huashan 1914 Creative Park, Pier-2 Art Center

### 🤖 AI Training Data and Open Data

- **[Taiwan AI Training Corpus (TAIC)](https://taic.moda.gov.tw)**: Promoted by the Ministry of Digital Affairs, collecting high-quality Traditional Chinese language materials from central government agencies. Features 3,000+ datasets, 1.1+ billion tokens, covering six categories: culture and arts, language and vocabulary, historical artifacts, local culture, tourism and travel, and educational learning. Available for enterprise and academic applications. Second half of 2026 will expand collaboration with private sector.
- **Government Platforms**: Government Data Open Platform (data.gov.tw), Government Portal
- **Legal Databases**: Laws & Regulations Database
- **Civic Participation**: g0v (gov-zero) civic tech community
- **Cultural Archives**: Digital Taiwan, National Cultural Memory Bank
