# Founder — Che-Yu Wu

## About Che-Yu

**Che-Yu Wu** is a new media artist, founder of MonoLab (Monoame Interactive Design), and the initiator of Taiwan.md.

## International Art Experience

His works have been exhibited at major international art platforms:
- **Venice Biennale**
- **Art Basel Miami**
- **Cannes Immersive Market**
- **Artist Residency at Cent Quatre 104, Paris**

## Representative Works

- **The Formula of Everything** (Taipei 101 Exhibition) — World's largest p5.js generative art exhibition
- **Soul Fish** (Venice Biennale) — Exploring collective intelligence of digital life
- **Soul of Flowers** (Art Basel Miami) — Algorithmic aesthetics of life cycles
- **The Eternal Garden in the Bottle** (Paris Residency) — Digital ecosystem in a time capsule
- **Algorithmic Poetry** (Immersive Piano Performance) — Real-time symbiosis of sound and visual
- **Coffee Dreamscape** (Starbucks DreamPlaza) — Artistic transformation of commercial space

## Educational Impact

Che-Yu is not only a creator but also a knowledge shairer:
- **Hahow Online Courses**: Over 22,000 students
- **Former Lecturer at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University**: Generative Interactive Art and System Design
- **TouchDesigner Workshop**: Promoting interactive art technology

## Core Philosophy

> "I am an ancient clockmaker, the mechanism itself is the artwork"

For Che-Yu, algorithmic art isn't about making computers draw, but about designing rules for life. He believes true art lies in creating systems that give artworks their own logic of existence.

## Why Taiwan.md?

At international art venues, Che-Yu was constantly asked: "What is Taiwan like?"

Each time, he wished he could point to a complete, heartfelt portal that would let foreign friends truly understand Taiwan — not just night markets and xiaolongbao, but the rich culture, innovative energy, and people's way of life on this land.

But such a place didn't exist.

So Taiwan.md was born.
